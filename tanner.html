I began my career in art as an illustrator, which you can see reflected in my tattoo style. My work is highly detailed, imaginative, and often freehand (drawn directly onto the skin with sharpie) to create a design that organically flows with the body. I enjoy tattooing imagery that is abstract, esoteric, and surreal.

The best part about tattooing is connecting with new people through art and creating a safe, accepting container for the client to enjoy their experience!
