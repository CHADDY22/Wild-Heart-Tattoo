Hey, I’m Francis Cassidy. I’ve been tattooing for almost 5 years now, but my love for art goes back as far as I can remember. While I’m comfortable working in just about any style, I specialize in traditional, illustrative, and ornamental designs—bold, clean lines with a touch of detail that makes each piece stand out.

I pride myself on keeping things laid-back and making sure everyone who sits in my chair feels comfortable and cared for. Whether you’re getting your first tattoo or adding to your collection, I’m here to work with you and create something that’s uniquely yours.

Come by Wild Heart Tattoo, and let’s turn your idea into something you’ll wear proudly forever.
