BOOKING . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
How to book with an artist . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
Please reach out to the artist directly to book. All of our artists do their own booking. If you’re unsure who you’d like to book with please check out the Artists page with all examples of their work and feel free to reach out to them directly to see if your tattoo idea is something they would like to take on.
Deposits and shop minimum . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .  
Our shop minimum for a tattoo is $200. All tattoo appointments require a $100 non-refundable deposit. For larger or multi-session pieces, a higher deposit may be required. With at least 48 hours notice, our artists are happy to reschedule your appointment during their next available booking period.
Group appointments . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
For group appointments, deposits are treated separately. If a member of the group drops out, their deposit is not transferable to anyone else in the group.
no-call-no-shows . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
Our artists work hard, and spend a lot of time not only on tattooing, but also on scheduling, preparation, and cleanup. If you no-call-no-show, you are not respecting our artists’ time, and therefore will not be considered for rebooking with any artist at Wild Heart.
ETIQUETTE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
TREAT EACH OTHER WELL . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
Wild Heart is an inclusive studio for all people - and to promote a safe and comfortable environment for all, We ask that you treat each other well. We absolutely do not tolerate racism, sexism, homophobia, bigotry or hatred.
BRINGING GUESTS . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . 
Please reach out to your artist if you plan on bringing a guest. With the size of our space we can’t always guarantee there will be room and we love for everyone to be comfortable. If you are bringing a guest and the shop is full, we have a ton of good recommendations for places they can check out within walking distance of the shop. Places we recommend are Cake Life, Persimmon Coffee, La Colombe, 3 J’s Food Market, Pizza Shackamaxon.
18+ ONLY . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . 
As a shop policy, we will not tattoo anyone under the age of 18 - regardless of parental consent - no exceptions.

You will receive written aftercare instructions at your appointment and if you are not experienced with taking care of a new tattoo our artists are more than happy to guide you.
