Hi! I’m Maribeth! I’m a jack of all trades, but I  specialize in medieval engraving style tattoos as well as fineline tattoos, scientific illustration, and botanical work.

I started tattooing at a walk-in shop on the Jersey Shore, so I am able to do most styles (including color!) except realism.

My favorite things to tattoo are creatures of any kind (especially dragons, birds, snakes, and bunnies!) and I have a lot of repeatable flash designs to choose from.

If you have any questions or want to book an appointment, feel free to send me a message on instagram!

Can’t wait to meet you!
